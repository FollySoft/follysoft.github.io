Perspectives Game

How to Play

Find a space (indoor or outdoor) where you're willing to spend some time with a camera. Open this page and press the start button to be assigned a random creature. For the next 10 minutes, use your camera to try and see the space as if through through this creature's eyes. Some rules will be provided, but feel free to ignore them!


Ready to move on to a different perspective? Press the reshuffle button!

Bat

Flip your camera upside down (if your camera doesnt let you turn it upside down, just flip the pic after the fact).

Bird

Take pictures looking down at something from a high angle.

Flower

Lay your phone (or yourself) flat on its back, taking photos looking straight up.

Gecko

The bottom of your camera must be touching a surface (wall, ceiling, rock, etc).

Goldfish

Take pictures through an additional lens (glass window, sheet of plastic, glass of water, reflections, etc.).

Mouse

Take pictures as if you are peering out from a hiding place (hole in a fence, crack in a door, behind a tree, etc).
