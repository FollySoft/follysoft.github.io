Perspectives Game

How to Play

Find a space (indoor or outdoor) where you're willing to spend some time with a camera. Open this page and press the start button to be assigned a random creature. For the next 10 minutes, use your camera to image seeing the space through this creature's eyes. Some guidelines and tips for each creature's perspective will be provided, but feel free to ignore them!


Ready to move on to a different perspective? Press the play button again to reshuffle!
