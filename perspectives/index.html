Perspectives Game

How to Play

Find a space (indoor or outdoor) where you're willing to spend some time with a camera. Open this page and press the PLAY button to be assigned a random creature. For the next 10 minutes, use your camera to try and see the space as if through this creature's eyes. Some suggestions of how to accomplish this will be provided, but feel free to ignore them!


Ready to move on to a different perspective? Press the reshuffle button!

There's currently 6 creatures to try: bat, bird, flower, gecko, goldfish, and mouse!

Take pictures as a...

Bat

Need a suggestion?
  • Flip your camera upside down (if your camera doesnt let you turn it upside down, just flip the pic after the fact).
  • Take pictures with your eyes closed.

Bird

Need a suggestion? Take pictures looking down at something from a high angle.

Flower

Need a suggestion?
  • Lay your phone (or yourself) flat on its back, taking photos looking straight up.
  • Make sure the sun is in every picture you take.

Gecko

Need a suggestion? The bottom of your camera must be touching a surface (wall, ceiling, rock, etc).

Goldfish

Need a suggestion? Take pictures through an additional lens (glass window, sheet of plastic, glass of water, reflections, etc.).

Mouse

Need a suggestion? Take pictures as if you are peering out from a hiding place (hole in a fence, crack in a door, behind a tree, etc).

Excitable Dog

Need a suggestion?
  • Take a picture os something while the camera is in motion.
  • Get your camera within "sniffing distance" of something.

Tourist

Need a suggestion?
  • Take a picture of yourself posing with anything of interest.
